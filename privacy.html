Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Love Machine Inc. ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in connection with our AI-powered content creation services.

1. Information We Collect

Our application collects only the minimum information necessary to function:

  • OAuth tokens: When you authorize our app to post on your behalf, we receive access tokens from third-party platforms (e.g., TikTok). These tokens are stored securely and used solely to publish content to your account.
  • Platform user IDs: Your public user ID on connected platforms, used to identify your account for content publishing.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or browsing history through our application.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Publish AI-generated content to your authorized social media accounts.
  • Retrieve engagement metrics (views, likes, comments) for content optimization.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties, except as required by law or to comply with platform API terms.

4. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens are stored in encrypted server-side storage and are never exposed to client-side code or third parties. We follow industry-standard security practices.

5. Data Retention

We retain OAuth tokens only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may revoke access at any time through the respective platform's settings, which will invalidate our stored tokens.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: info@lovemachine.jp
